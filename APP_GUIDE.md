# Violin Tracker - Premium Studio Management

Welcome to your revamped **Violin Tracker**! This guide covers how to use the new premium interface to manage your students, schedule, and attendance logging.

## 1. Navigating the App
You can now customize how you navigate:
- **Sidebar Mode**: Fixed on the left (best for large screens/desktop).
- **Bottom Navigation Mode**: Mobile-friendly bar at the bottom.
- **Change View**: Go to **Settings** > **Display & View** to toggle your preference.

## 2. Student Registry (`/students`)
- **Add Student**: Use the golden "Add Student" button to enroll new studio members.
- **Search**: Quickly find students by name or level using the search bar.
- **Profile**: Click any student card to view their full history and schedule.

## 3. Managing a Student
On the **Student Detail** page, you can:
- **Log Attendance**: Quickly mark a session as Present, Absent, or Excused.
- **Edit Profile**: Update student name, level, email, or timezone.
- **Schedule Lesson**: Add recurring weekly lesson slots.
- **Unregister**: Remove a student from your studio (this also deletes their history).

## 4. Attendance & Logs (`/sessions`)
- **View All Logs**: See a complete archive of every class you've taught.
- **Filter**: Use the search bar to find records for a specific student or look through your session notes.
- **Details**: Each entry shows the date, status, and any notes you recorded during the lesson.

## 5. Dashboard Overview (`/`)
- Get a quick snapshot of your studio's health.
- Track total students, attendance rates, and missed sessions for the week.
- Access recent student profiles with one click.
